MESSAGES
==========

### menu-item-clicked
sender: background 
receiver: extension
example: `{type: 'menuitem-clicked', data: clickData}`

### perform-action
sender: popup
receiver: webpage
example: `{type: 'perform-action', data: {}}`

## wepbage-contextmenu
sender: webpage
receiver: extension
example: `{type: 'webpage-contextmenu', data: {selector: '#my-id'}}`
